# Essential Phone

A smartphone startup founded by Android creator Andy Rubin. The Essential Phone was praised for design but launched with a terrible camera, high price, and was soon overshadowed by Rubin's sexual misconduct allegations.

## The Rise

**2015-11** — Andy Rubin leaves Google; founds Essential Products

**2017-05** — Essential Phone unveiled with titanium body and edge-to-edge display

**2017-08** — Launches at $699; reviewers praise design but pan camera

## The Fall

**2017-10** — Price dropped to $499 after poor sales

**2017-11** — The Information reports Andy Rubin left Google after sexual misconduct investigation

**2018-10** — Company valued at $1.2B but has sold fewer than 90,000 phones

**2020-02** — Announces closure; never shipped the planned Essential Phone 2

## Key Lessons

### Founder reputation is product reputation

When Rubin's misconduct history surfaced, Essential's brand was toxic. No amount of design excellence could overcome it.

### Camera quality is non-negotiable in smartphones

The Essential Phone had a beautiful titanium body but a terrible camera. In 2017, camera quality was the primary smartphone differentiator.

### Being first doesn't matter if you're not best

Essential beat Apple to the bezel-less design. But Apple executed it better with Face ID and a working camera.
