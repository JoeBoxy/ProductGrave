# Neeva

A subscription-based search engine founded by the former head of Google's advertising business. Promised an ad-free, privacy-respecting search experience but couldn't convince users to pay for something Google offered for free.

## The Rise

**2019-01** — Founded by ex-Google ad boss Sridhar Ramaswamy and Vivek Raghunathan

**2021-06** — Launches in US with $4.95/month subscription

**2022-01** — Expands to Europe; adds free tier with limited searches

## The Fall

**2022-10** — Raises $50M Series B; total funding reaches $77M

**2023-01** — Announces pivot to AI search and enterprise products

**2023-05** — Shuts down consumer search; sells consumer business to Snowflake

## Key Lessons

### Users won't pay for search

Google has trained two generations of users that search is free. Overcoming that expectation requires a dramatically better product, not just 'no ads.'

### Distribution beats product quality in search

Neeva's search results were good. But Google is the default on every browser and phone. Distribution is everything in search.

### Even ex-Google executives can't beat Google

Sridhar Ramaswamy ran Google's $100B ad business. If he couldn't build a viable competitor with $77M, the search market is truly closed.
