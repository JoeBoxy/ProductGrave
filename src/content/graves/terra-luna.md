# Terra / Luna

A blockchain ecosystem built around algorithmic stablecoin UST and its sister token LUNA. At its peak, Terra's tokens were worth $60B. Collapsed to near-zero in May 2022 when UST lost its dollar peg, wiping out billions in value.

## The Rise

**2018-01** — Terraform Labs founded by Do Kwon and Daniel Shin in South Korea

**2020-09** — UST stablecoin launches with 20% yield on Anchor Protocol

**2021-03** — LUNA reaches $20; ecosystem growing rapidly

**2022-04** — LUNA hits all-time high of $119; UST market cap over $18B

## The Fall

**2022-05-07** — $2B UST withdrawn from Anchor Protocol over 48 hours

**2022-05-09** — UST depegs from $1; death spiral begins

**2022-05-13** — LUNA crashes from $80 to under $0.0001; UST worth $0.17

**2022-09** — Interpol issues Red Notice for Do Kwon

**2023-03** — Do Kwon arrested in Montenegro using forged documents

## Key Lessons

### Algorithmic stablecoins are inherently unstable

UST wasn't backed by real assets. It relied on arbitrage with LUNA. When panic selling started, the 'algorithm' amplified the collapse instead of stopping it.

### 20% guaranteed yield is a red flag

Anchor Protocol promised 20% APY on UST. The yield was subsidized by Terraform Labs to attract users. It was unsustainable and everyone knew it.

### Crypto hubris destroys real lives

Retail investors put life savings into UST based on Do Kwon's assurances. The collapse caused suicides, bankruptcies, and destroyed families in South Korea.
